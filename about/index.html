About

Hello, my name is Ken Chen and I am a Gameplay Programmer, and sometimes Game Designer and 2D Artist. Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at Queens College NY and teaching Unity classes at Queens College's Tech Incubator.

My main interests are creating gameplay and making certain mechanics feel good and fun to use, like shooting, slashing, and movement. To me, these basic actions deserve all the attention it needs, because they are used repeatedly throughout the game.

I like to work on anything gameplay related and building systems that works well together.
