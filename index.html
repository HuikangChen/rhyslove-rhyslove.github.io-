Game Description:

A 2D side scrolling Endless Runner. The player is a deer running through a forest collecting coins and solving multi-operation math problems.

Developed and published MathDeer on Google Play Store. Created all of the art assets with Aesprite. Designed and programmed all the gameplay and systems with Unity.
